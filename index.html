Why Green Jobs Matter?

Our project investigates how the next generation can protect the planet through professional careers.

Conservation

Protecting existing ecosystems and restoring degraded lands to ensure biodiversity survives for centuries.

Renewable Energy

Transitioning the world to clean energy sources like wind, solar, and hydro to fight climate change.

Circular Economy

Designing products and systems that eliminate waste and keep resources in use as long as possible.
